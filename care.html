Care Instructions

Each MARA piece is made from premium crepe, Japanese silk blends, matte satin, or structured nida — fabrics that reward considered care.

01

Dry clean only

All MARA pieces are dry clean only. Avoid machine and hand washing, which can distort the drape and embroidery.

02

Protect the embroidery

Turn embroidered pieces inside out before pressing. Use a cool iron with a cloth barrier — never iron directly over hand-finished detailing.

03

Store on the shoulder

Hang on a padded or wide hanger to preserve the shoulder line. Keep away from direct sunlight to protect colour and sheen.

04

Handle fragrance & oils

Apply perfume and oils before dressing and let them dry — satin and silk blends mark easily.
